## Problem

Operationalizing AI models, automated agents, and intelligent workflows requires real-time visibility into performance, data quality, and downstream business impact. The operational data needed for that visibility was fragmented across CRM, project, procurement, finance, task, and event systems. Unified reporting required CSV exports, manual pivot tables, and emailed spreadsheets, which made reports stale before anyone could act on them.

When an AI-driven metric or critical operational KPI looked wrong, leadership could not drill into the underlying record path directly. That created a gap for monitoring system health, investigating anomaly signals, and understanding whether automated workflows were improving or degrading performance.
  </div>
  <div>
## Solution

I designed and built a real-time AI/MLOps observability dashboard that aggregates operational records into configurable, role-based KPI views. The platform turns fragmented business systems into a single intelligence layer for monitoring AI system performance, detecting operational anomalies, and giving leadership current decision support without requiring manual report assembly.

## How It Works

<HowItWorksGrid>
<HowItWorksCard title="Cross-Module Aggregation for AI-Ready Data">
CRM, Sales, Projects, Vendor Management, and Accounting records are queried through a unified data layer, creating a cohesive dataset for MLOps-style monitoring without manual exports or stale imports.
</HowItWorksCard>

<HowItWorksCard title="Server-Side Metric Computation">
Metrics are computed at the database and ORM layer using dynamic domain filtering, which keeps performance consistent while supporting real-time AI model, agent workflow, and operational KPI views.
</HowItWorksCard>

<HowItWorksCard title="Purpose-Built Observability Views">
Personal tasks, team workload, upcoming engagements, pipeline health, vendor operations, revenue metrics, and a customizable canvas each expose a focused view into human and AI-driven process performance.
</HowItWorksCard>

<HowItWorksCard title="Interactive Anomaly Drill-Down">
Line, bar, doughnut, pie, radar, scatter, and geographic map visualizations let users click KPI tiles or chart segments and drill directly into live records for root cause analysis.
</HowItWorksCard>

<HowItWorksCard title="Role-Based Layouts">
GridStack.js powers drag-and-drop dashboards where layout, filters, and widget configuration are saved per user, letting each role monitor the AI, agent, and operational signals they own.
</HowItWorksCard>

<HowItWorksCard title="Dynamic Time-Window Analysis">
Saved filter presets allow teams to switch date ranges instantly, compare periods, inspect trend shifts, and investigate anomaly patterns without rebuilding reports.
</HowItWorksCard>

<HowItWorksCard title="Scheduled Intelligence Delivery">
Dashboards auto-email chart snapshots to defined recipients on a recurring schedule, giving leadership proactive operational intelligence without requiring a dashboard login.
</HowItWorksCard>

<HowItWorksCard title="Multi-Format Reporting Engine">
One-click export to PDF, Excel, and PNG produces executive-ready outputs for AI performance reviews, operational reviews, and MLOps reporting without manual formatting.
</HowItWorksCard>

<HowItWorksCard title="Modular Architecture for Scaling Insights">
New KPI widgets and AI-driven insight modules can be added without modifying core infrastructure, while migration scripts and tests protect data integrity, security rules, and access control.
</HowItWorksCard>
</HowItWorksGrid>
