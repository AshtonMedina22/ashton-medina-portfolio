## Problem

After a sale, operations faced a critical bottleneck: hours spent manually rebuilding client details, recreating task lists, assigning teams, and chasing requirements already captured in the CRM. This manual gap between "sold" and "started" created:

- Data fragmentation and quality issues, making sales data unreliable for analytics or future AI systems.
- Costly delivery delays and inaccurate scope or resource allocation.
- Limited traceability, making it difficult to audit the journey from sale to execution.

The business needed an MLOps-style data pipeline to automate the handoff, protect data integrity, trigger immediate execution, and create a trusted source of truth for operational planning and future AI insights.
  </div>
  <div>
## Solution

I designed and implemented a production-grade MLOps-ready data pipeline and workflow orchestration system that instantly converts confirmed sales into fully scoped, delivery-ready projects. The solution automates project generation, inherits client requirements directly from the CRM, and keeps CRM, sales, projects, tasks, vendors, and financial records synchronized. It also provides end-to-end traceability from initial customer contact to project closeout, creating a reliable AI-ready data foundation for analytics and future intelligent automation.

## How It Works

<HowItWorksGrid>
<HowItWorksCard title="Order Validation & Data Quality Gate">
Validates order structure on confirmation and blocks malformed records before downstream automation, MLOps pipelines, reporting, or future AI systems consume dirty operational data.
</HowItWorksCard>

<HowItWorksCard title="CRM Source of Truth Linkage">
Creates or links the sales opportunity and maps core deal fields, establishing the canonical record that downstream objects, automations, and MLOps data pipelines reference.
</HowItWorksCard>

<HowItWorksCard title="Automated Project & Task Orchestration">
Confirmation automatically generates the project workspace and copies task templates per service line, with each product spawning a task tree dynamically scoped to the confirmed sale.
</HowItWorksCard>

<HowItWorksCard title="Unified Task Graph & Fragmentation Control">
Merges generated tasks into one unified project record, reducing the fragmentation native ERP flows create on multi-service orders and producing cleaner data for observability.
</HowItWorksCard>

<HowItWorksCard title="Bidirectional Data Sync Engine">
Keeps 65+ critical fields consistent across CRM, Sales Orders, Contacts, and Projects, creating a unified MLOps-ready dataset for reporting, analytics, and future feature engineering.
</HowItWorksCard>

<HowItWorksCard title="CRM Stage Automation & Audit Trail">
Drives CRM stage progression from key milestones and logs timestamped audit entries to the record thread, supporting governance, compliance, and debugging of pipeline anomalies.
</HowItWorksCard>
